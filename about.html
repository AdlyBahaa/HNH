About POLARIS

 

We are an Egyptian System Integrator company, we have been delivering exceptional solutions since our establishment in 2012. Our primary focus is on providing smart solutions with remarkable quality, time delivery, and competitive pricing. POLARIS has established a reputation for fulfilling contractual obligations and providing excellent customer service. Our approach is working closely with clients and team members has enabled us to achieve success in a smart, efficient, and effective manner. With over 10 years of experience in various industries(mainly in the hospitality sector), doubled with our extensive knowledge and expertise in the hospitality sector, we have become a renowned entity in our field.

 

Milestone

POLARIS was founded in 2012 with an excellent start with the passive works until doing partnerships with significant brand that assist our work in passive and active.

 

Passive

We are one of the leading companies in the field of civil works for lowcurrent systems, specializing in all aspects of pathway construction, cable pulling, and restoration with utmost professionalism. Our progress is driven by a dedicated team of network experts skilled in handling cables and executing all low-current projects efficiently.

Active

POLARIS has been developed to include the supply of switches, wireless systems, and surveillance systems. We have established partnerships with leading global brands in the networking field to combine our experience in network construction with the ability to identify and provide the most suitable operational network equipment.
